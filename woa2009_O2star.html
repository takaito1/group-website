WOA2009

O2* is defined as O2 + R PO4 and is a quasi-conservative tracer, and is known to correlate with temperature (Keeling and Garcia, 2002). Correlation and regression coefficients are calculated at 0m, 50m and 100m depths and are saved as a MATLAB file.
