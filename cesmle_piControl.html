CESM-LE pre-industrial control

Dissolved O2 fields from the 200 years of piControl are correlated with various climate indices. The O2 model output is interpolated onto global 1x1 long-lat grid on annual timescale. The climate indices are taken from CVDP and averaged into annual timeseries (image), (.mat file download), and the correlation coefficients are calculated. The analysis window is Year 1900 to 2099. Correlation coefficients are calculated at 100m, 200m and 400m depths (.mat file download).
