about us

Our goal is to contribute to the fundamental understanding of the Earth's biogeochemical cycling in the present and past climate. We, together with our collaborators, conduct research in Ocean Carbon Cycle, Dissolved Oxygen, and Machine Learning applications to the ocean biogeochemistry.
